# Hello Orias — Product Overview

## Product
**Name**: Hello Orias
**Type**: Personal landing page
**Scope**: Single-section, minimal

## What It Is
A clean, single-section landing page that opens with a bold "Hello Orias" greeting. Minimal by design — one headline, one message, one page. No navigation, no clutter, nothing extra.

## Users / Audience
- **Primary**: Orias — personal digital presence, a simple online introduction

## Positioning
The simplest possible online presence: one section, one greeting, zero noise. Fast to load, easy to share, impossible to get wrong.

## Brand & Tone
- Calm, clean, personal
- Typography-first aesthetic
- Dark accents on white — minimal contrast-driven design
- No complexity, no distractions

## Core Content
- **Hero section**: "Hello Orias" as the bold main headline
- Supporting subheadline: "Selamat datang."
- Single full-viewport section, centred layout

## Strategic Principles
1. **Simplicity is the feature** — never add complexity for its own sake
2. **The greeting is the product** — the headline carries all the weight
3. **Fast and shareable** — instant load, no barriers, one link

## Weekly Verified Operations
- **Operation type**: Page visit / greeting received
- **Volume assumption**: Assuming ~20 visitors/week (personal sharing via social, messaging)
- **Formula**: 20 visitors/week × 1 greeting each = **20 verified greetings / week**
- **Value per operation**: Personal brand presence — qualitative; no direct revenue modelled
